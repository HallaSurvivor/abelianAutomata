\section{Z[x] Module}
In the case of interest, $\G \cong \Z^m$. Further, $\del_0$ extends to 
a $\frac{1}{2}$-Integral matrix $\Am$ of irreducible character. Then
$\G$ admits representation as a $\Z[x]$ module where 
$x \cdot \v = \Am^{-1}\v$, extended linearly.
